Questions for Investigation¶

  1. How did salary change over the years?
  2. Which team has the highest average salary? And the lowest?
  3. Which is the most well paid player at any given time?
  4. Which statistics have most influence on salaries?

Dataset¶

The 2016 version of The Lahman Baseball Database contains complete batting and pitching statistics from 1871 to 2016, plus fielding statistics, standings, team stats, managerial records, post-season data, and more. The Master (player names, DOB, and biographical info), Pitching (regular season pitching statistics), Fielding (regular season fielding statistics), Batting (regular season batting statistics), Salaries (players salaries data) and Teams (yearly stats and standings) tables are required for this balk analysis.

Data Wrangling¶

Missing Data¶

Since the salaries table contains data from 1985 to 2016, we have to exclude all years before 1985 from our analysis.

Merging Tables¶

To obtain correlations between all the baseball statistics we have to merge the master, batting, fielding, pitching, and salaries tables.

def corr_per_group(df, column1, column2, group):
    """
    Return correlations between column1 and column2 grouped by group
    """
    grouped_df = df.groupby(group)
    corr_df = grouped_df[[column1,column2]].corr()
    idx_slice = pd.IndexSlice
    values = corr_df.loc[idx_slice[:,column1], idx_slice[column2]]
    clean_values = values.dropna()
    return clean_values
